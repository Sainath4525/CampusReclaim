How CampusReclaim Works

Reclaiming your items has never been easier. Just 3 simple steps.

1. Report

Fill in details, choose a category, pick the campus zone, and upload an image to post your item report.

2. Match & Search

Browse through reported lost or found items using our smart real-time location and category filters.

3. Reclaim

Click "I Found This!" or verify your ownership to safely get in touch and arrange a secure hand-off.
